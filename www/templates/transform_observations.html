Equatorial to Horizontal

This transforms coordinates between equatorial and horizontal coordinate systems. If you know the right ascension and declination of your celestial object, for example from Sirius' wikipedia page, this will calculate the azimuth and altitude at the observer's location. Conversely, given the azimuth and altitude, this will calculate the right ascension and declination.
