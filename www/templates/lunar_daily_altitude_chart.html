Daily Sun and Moon Paths


This page shows the paths of the sun and moon as a plot of azimuth vs. altitude over a day starting at local midnight. This uses Jean Meeus' simplified algorithm for the moon position. He estimates accuracy at 10 arc-seconds in longitude and 4 arc-seconds in latitude. I am seeing even greater differences when comparing my results to Star Walk.

I do see a partial eclipse I observed but I don't have more precise data on that time. This shows there is one comming on so I will have a chance to see how accurate this is.

The sun and moon markers are not to scale.
