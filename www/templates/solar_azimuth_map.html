Solar Azimuth Map


This page will take an observer location and time and plot the sun rise (green), current position (yellow) and sun set (red) as markers on a google map. The most obvious test case is Stonehenge. shows the sun rise marker pointing towards the heal stone. shows the sun set marker pointing away from the heal stone. (The markers can be dragged without changing their bearing.) This gives a sunrise azimuth of 49:22 which is in good agreement with what I found on this astronomical archaeology site

Manhattanhenge (as noted by the New York Times and BBC) is another example, but since the streets are not alligned with the solstice sunrise, there are four alignments (and four half alignments)
